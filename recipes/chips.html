Homemade Chips

How to make crispy potato chips at home?

Make microwave potato chips with this easy recipe.
They are a tasty, easy, and economical alternative to store-bought potato chips.
A mandoline makes slicing the potato into paper-thin slices a breeze.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 potato, sliced paper thin (peel optional)
  • 1 potato, sliced paper thin (peel optional)
  • Â½ teaspoon salt, or to taste

Instructions/Steps

  1. Lightly grease a large dinner plate with cooking spray; set aside.
  2. Pour oil into a plastic bag (a produce bag works well). Add potato slices and shake to coat.
    Arrange potato slices in a single layer on the prepared plate.
  3. Cook in the microwave on high until lightly browned, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove
    chips to a large bowl and toss with salt. Chips will crisp as they cool. Repeat
    with remaining potato slices; no need to grease the plate again.
  4. Enjoy!
